Our Mission

NigelEnergy Consulting provides energy solutions to clients and assist its clients in addressing their Greenhouse Gas emissions (GHG) and sustainability concerns.

Our expertise is to ensure our clients get energy 24 hours in a decarbonized environment while keeping energy costs down.

Services

Energy Audits

We start our clients with an analysis of energy consumption and benchmarking with others (peers), followed by a walk through, energy analytics and the generation of report.

Renewable Energy

With the world on a carbon diet, we address our clients operational energy cost by switching to a renewable sources to improve their energy security.

Sustainability Consulting

Our sustainability program is a collective effort not to be attempted alone by the well-meaning. We assist our clients with LEED, LEAF, Waste Management and other programs to address the overall effectiveness of green projects.

Energy Efficiency

We design tailored energy solutions to assist our clients in reducing operating costs in their energy use.

GHG

As climate policy, we assist our clients in reducing their GHG emissions.

Solar – Reversible Shades

Why pay for a view you cannot view? We assist our clients to improve visibility, daylighting and 80% increased window insulation.
